FERRARI

Ferrari S.p.A. (/fəˈrɑːri/; Italian: [ferˈraːri]) is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer based in Maranello, Italy. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 the company built its first car in 1940, and produced its first Ferrari-badged car in 1947.
